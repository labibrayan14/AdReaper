Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AdReaper. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to make this absolutely clear: AdReaper does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not track your browsing history, your IP address, or any other identifiable information.

3. Local Processing Only

All filtering and blocking happens locally in your browser. There is no communication with external servers. This ensures your data stays private and secure.

4. No Ads, No Trackers

AdReaper is built to protect you from ads and trackers, not to become one. We will never inject ads or harvest your data.

5. Third-Party Services

AdReaper does not rely on any third-party analytics or advertising services. You're fully protected within the scope of this extension.

6. Permissions

The permissions requested by the extension are strictly necessary for its functionality. We do not misuse these permissions in any way.

7. Changes to This Policy
