AdReaper Privacy Policy

Introduction

At AdReaper, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our ad-blocking extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information from our users. AdReaper operates locally on your device and does not track your browsing activity. We do not share or sell any data to third parties.

How We Use Your Data

Since AdReaper does not collect any personal data, we do not use, store, or share any of your data. The extension solely functions to block ads and enhance your browsing experience without accessing any sensitive information.

Data Security

As we do not collect or store any user data, there are no security concerns related to data storage. However, we do recommend that you regularly update your browser and extension for improved security.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there are no rights to access, modify, or delete data. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us through our support page.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any significant changes.
